On November 28-29, 2024, the international conference Country and World: Russian Realities-2024 will take place. It continues a series of annual conferences, which in 2018-2021 were held at the Moscow Sakharov Center. Since 2023, the conference has become international.

We will talk about the aggressive war that Russia has been waging for two and a half years, and about what is happening in Russia itself against the backdrop of this war. Political scientists, sociologists, economists, and international relations specialists will discuss the transformation of the political regime and its ideology, unstable wartime and the effectiveness of the sanctions policy of Western society, the state of Russia and repression within the country.

Among the conference participants are Kirill Rogov, Ekaterina Shulman, Alexander Morozov, Maria Snegovaya, Andrey Yakovlev, Pavel Luzin, Alexey Levinson and others.

The conference will take place in Berlin, but you can follow us online. If you want to listen to speakers in Russian, join the broadcast on the Country and World Youtube-channel. If you need an English translation, please register here and we will send you a zoom link.

If you would like to attend the conference in person, we ask you to fill out the registration form. Unfortunately, the number of seats in the hall is limited, so we cannot invite everyone. We will definitely send you confirmation of registration and information about the exact location of the conference if we are able to host you.
